About me

I'm Barbara and I am a

I was born in Brazil, but moved to Oman in 2009. There, I studied at TAISM (The American International School of Muscat). In 2013, my family moved back to Brazil, where I got a scholarship to study at EABH (American School of Belo Horizonte).

I stayed at EABH until my Sophomore year, then transferred to Coleguium, a highly sought-after Brazilian school. For my Junior year, I passed in a 3-step selective process done by Rotary and spent 1 year abroad in Belgium. There, I studied at Institut Valleé Bailly. Once I was back in Brazil, I graduated through CESEC and applied at PUC Minas, a school nominated by Folha de São Paulo as one of the top 5 private schools in the country. I was accepted and am now coursing Computer Science.

During the pandemic (before college), I worked freelance as an artist and colorist. Some of my works include being a colorist for two published comics written by Samuel Vera. You can see my work in the "art" tab.
